Products > ARGUS Valuation - DCF
 

ARGUS Valuation - DCF™ is the industry standard commercial real estate cash flow projection, transaction analysis and asset valuation solution.

For over 20 years, ARGUS Valuation-DCF™ has empowered agents, investors, lenders and other real estate professionals to spend more of their valuable time building fortunes, not spreadsheets. ARGUS Valuation-DCF is currently available in German, French, Spanish, Chinese and Japanese, and incorporates many local valuation methodologies.

Over 20,000 users worldwide rely on ARGUS Software™ to:

  • Quickly and accurately analyze commercial real estate assets and transactions
  • Understand market realities and risks
  • Develop leasing strategies, business and marketing plans
  • Forecast property cash flows
  • Calculate investment values and returns
  • Share asset and transactional data with clients, partners and colleagues
  • Navigate complex international deal structures
  • ARGUS Valuation-DCF™ provides customized dashboard visibility into properties and portfolios with printable executive summary.
  • Global Interoperability
    Work on more cross-border investment projects with the global capabilities in ARGUS Valuation-DCF. In addition to the ability to create default settings by country, the results in ARGUS Valuation-DCF may be instantaneously translated from English, French, German, Spanish or Japanese into any of the above stated languages.

  • Global standard
    ARGUS Valuation-DCF is the recognized standard for national and international agents, lenders, asset managers, pension advisors, and investors. Using ARGUS Valuation-DCF will provide more efficient access to these players.

  • Powers critical business processes
    ARGUS Valuation-DCF not only powers the critical business processes and workflows that drive commercial real estate, but also provides a market-tested, consistent standard for commercial real estate organizations.

  • Fast and powerful
    ARGUS Valuation-DCF is a sophisticated application that saves valuable time with its unique style of flexible tenancy schedule input, drill-down capabilities, calculation engine and dashboard interface.

  • Accurate and reliable
    ARGUS Valuation-DCF has been carefully engineered and tested by real estate and programming professionals, and has been market tested by thousands of global companies.
  • Understandable and audit worthy
    ARGUS Valuation-DCF documents your assumptions and supports your financial conclusions with its standard reporting of all input data, property and portfolio level summary reports and detailed tenant-by-tenant supporting schedules. ARGUS Valuation-DCF further provides reliable transparent results that are easily understood and dissected by reviewers.

  • Integrated and open
    ARGUS Valuation-DCF supports a number of data transfer tools and utilities, including an Excel Import Wizard, native reXML support for downloads from MRI and Yardi, RealmX for ARGUS Property Management integration, OpenARGUS integration with MS Access or SQL, and a robust Excel export tool and embedded Excel Report Writer.

From dirt to disposition, ARGUS Valuation - DCF quickly models simple or complex office, retail, industrial, multifamily, senior-assisted living, hotel, mixed-use and development projects. It performs financial projections, valuation and return calculations via a number of flexible, yet easy-to-use, features:

Feature Purposes

Executive Dashboard

Provides project snapshots with information relevant to the different roles within the commercial real estate industry such as appraisers, brokers and lenders.

Property Library Table

Organizes large portfolios or multiple versions of financial models for individual assets for easy access and comparative analysis

ARGUS Valuation - DCF Wizard

Streamlines the input of simple projects and shortens the learning curve

Rent Roll

Provides a flexible, intuitive interface for analyzing leases, speculative renewals and the absorption of vacant space

Space Absorption

Redemise existing space or ratably lease vacant or newly constructed space with ease

Monthly Revenue and Expense Details

Precisely forecast operating and capital budgets for individual projects or at a portfolio level

Global Input Assumption Categories

Standardize market rates, growth rates and unique tenant attributes across an organization to facilitate rapid projection updates and portfolio slicing and dicing

Excel Report Writer and Standard Reports

Create custom or ad-hoc reports in Excel with data dynamically linked to ARGUS Valuation - DCF files or access over 200 presentation reports, supporting schedules and audit tracking documents to support your financial results

Graphical Stacking Plan

Create a graphical representation of your building color coded by rent, expiration, size or a number of other methods

Portfolio Roll-up and Sensitivity Scenarios

Consolidate any number or type of projects at a tenant, property or partner level. Create detailed sensitivity scenarios to see the impact of changes to any number of assumption variables across a portfolio or to individual assets within a portfolio

Partnership Waterfall Calculations

Model equity contribution and distribution business rules in order to project financial returns to any number of partners within a project or portfolio; also model lender’s equity participation scenarios

Development Financing and Loan Sizing

Create draw notes tied directly to projected construction costs, refinancing scenarios tied to current or future period valuations, or mezzanine and permanent financing terms complete with points, fees, prepayment penalties, interest only periods and more (you can also model debt in an alternate currency).

Multi-Currency Support and Consolidation

Separate input and output currency settings and detailed exchange rate tables allow you to model individual assets in any local currency and then convert the financial results to any other currency at a project or portfolio level

International Lease Structures and Valuation

Powerful lease analysis techniques and tools for rent indexation and leasing cost conventions typical in North America, Europe and Asia.

Excel, Access and SQL Import and Export

Integrated tools and utilities for importing and exporting financial, tenant/unit, property and portfolio data to and from a range of business productivity applications.
